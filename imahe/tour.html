Top Tourist Spots in El Nido
Big Lagoon
Famous for its crystal-clear waters and dramatic limestone cliffs. Perfect for kayaking and sightseeing.
Small Lagoon
A hidden paradise surrounded by towering rocks, ideal for swimming and exploring by kayak.
Secret Beach
Accessible only by swimming through a small crevice, this secluded spot is a must-see for adventurers.
Shimizu Island
Known for its white sand beach and vibrant marine life, perfect for snorkeling and picnics.
Nacpan Beach
A long stretch of golden sand and clear waters, ideal for relaxing and enjoying the sunset.
Snake Island
Famous for its unique sandbar that appears during low tide, connecting the island to the mainland.
